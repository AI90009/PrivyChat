About PrivyChat

Why PrivyChat is different

PrivyChat is a secure messaging webchat designed for small and medium-sized enterprises. It was created to provide private internal communication through separate company workspaces and encrypted team messaging in a simple and professional environment.

Why PrivyChat was created

Many businesses rely on digital communication, but not all messaging platforms are designed with privacy, workspace separation, and SME simplicity in mind. PrivyChat was developed to demonstrate how a business-focused webchat can support secure communication while remaining accessible to employees and manageable for organisations.

What makes PrivyChat different

Separate company workspaces: Each organisation operates in its own dedicated environment.

Encrypted messaging: Message content is encrypted before storage to reduce plaintext exposure.

Privacy-first design: The platform focuses on message confidentiality rather than unnecessary extra features.

SME-friendly approach: The interface is clean, simple, and suitable for professional daily use.

How it aligns with the project

PrivyChat reflects the project aim of creating a secure web-based chat application for SMEs. Unlike general-purpose messaging systems, it focuses on company-based access control, internal team communication, and client-side encrypted message handling. This makes it more relevant to organisations that need a lightweight but privacy-aware communication platform.

Who it is for

PrivyChat is intended for SMEs that need a secure internal communication tool for employees. It is especially relevant for organisations handling business-sensitive discussions, internal coordination, or confidential workplace messaging.
